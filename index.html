About Me

Greetings! In this section I will be talking all about myself and this website.

Hello! My name is Mihir Kulshreshtha and I am a highschooler at Thomas Jefferson Highschool of Science and Technology.

This website was created by me in the summer of 2020. I came up with this idea after reading and reflecting on articles in Scienctific American Magazine. This magazine has articles focused on scientific and technological breakthroughs, and reading these articles fueled me to explore more ideas.

For example, while I was reading an article about how Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) can decrease probability of Alzheimers and other brain diseases. After reading this article, I researched more into CSF and it's healing capabilities. I found CSF is the nearest thing in our physiology to elixir. (See more of what I found under my biology tab)

After researching, I felt it was my scientific responsibility to share my findings and new ideas I have. Hence, you are now reading my website.
